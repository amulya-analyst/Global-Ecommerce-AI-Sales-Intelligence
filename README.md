## Project Overview
Global E-Commerce sales analysis with AI-powered sales forecasting 
and customer segmentation with Groq AI chatbot.

## Tools Used
- Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel
- Prophet (Sales Forecasting)
- KMeans Clustering (Customer Segmentation)
- Groq AI (Chatbot)
- Streamlit (Web App)

## AI Models
- Sales Forecast: Prophet model | Next 6 months prediction
- Customer Segmentation: KMeans | 3 customer groups
- AI Chatbot: Groq AI (llama3)
